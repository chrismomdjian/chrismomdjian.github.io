Projects

Safety Curriculum Needs Assessment

The Safety Curriculum Needs Assessment, Safety CNA for short, is a tool that allows NASA JPL employees and contractors to assign/unassign themselves to safety training within their internal LMS. It makes it easier for learners to understand what safety training they need to take based on their job duties.

HTML5 CSS3 Bootstrap Python 3+ Django MySQL Docker Kubernetes

Safety CNA Reporting Dashboard

The Reporting Dashboard works as a complementary tool to the Safety CNA. It pulls data from the SCNA and performs basic statistical analysis on the data to present it as useful information for senior management and safety engineers to view.

HTML5 CSS3 Bootstrap Datatables Chart.js MySQL
